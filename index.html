Catppuccin Coffee

An honest, cozy drink for those who value balance. A bold kick of caffeine stabilized with a touch of salt and warm cinnamon.

Recommended Tools

  • Espresso Capsule Machine: For a quick, consistent high-intensity base.
  • Electric Milk Frother: To achieve that thick, cloud-like foam texture.
  • Coffee Mug: A ceramic one works best to hold the heat.

Preparation Guide

Combine a double shot of strong espresso with a tiny pinch of salt to balance the bitterness. Top it with a generous layer of frothed milk and a dusting of cinnamon.

No fancy tools? No problem:

Use strong instant coffee with very little water for the base. For the foam, shake hot milk vigorously in a closed jar or whisk it by hand until bubbly.

NOTICE: This drink contains high caffeine levels and no added sugar. Not recommended for children under 12 or those sensitive to strong stimulants. Drink responsibly.
